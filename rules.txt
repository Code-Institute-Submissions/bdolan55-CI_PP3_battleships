

Rules


Battleship is a classic board game that involves a grid of squares representing the ocean. 
The objective of the game is to sink all of your opponent's ships within the 10 turns allowed.

Here are the basic rules of the Battleship game:

If a player's guess hits a any of the computers ship, it will be announced that the ship has been hit. 
The players grid will be marked with an "X" when a ship is hit and marked with "-" when it is a missed missile.

The player will guess up to 10 times to sink all 10 of the computers ships. 

In summary, Battleship is a game of strategy, deduction and a bit of luck. 
Players must try to guess the location of their opponent's ships. The game requires careful planning and strategic 
thinking to outsmart your opponent and emerge victorious.
